Our Mission

The AI Study Quiz 2025 was created to help students and learners discover which free AI tools best match their study habits. Whether you’re writing essays, solving math problems, summarizing long readings, or staying organized, artificial intelligence can support your learning in new and exciting ways.

Why AI Tools?

Tools such as ChatGPT, Claude, Perplexity AI, and Notion AI are already transforming how students study. This website was designed as an engaging way to explore which tool might fit your needs best — all while being free to try.

Who This Website Is For

This site is aimed at students, self-learners, and productivity seekers who want to experiment with AI without needing to pay for premium versions. It’s not an official website for any AI company; it’s simply a fun and informative project for educational purposes.
